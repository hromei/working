    Discover Iași is a start-up created in 2019, with the support of the Iași Local Council which it helped us get European funding. From 2020, we help tourists and locals to discover and rediscover Iasi in a way unique: using an electric bike! For this, we provide you with a fast, efficient and 100% sustainable means of transport that you can use to you travel around the city, both for tourism and personal purposes. In addition to bicycles, you we propose some pre-established routes that include some of the most beautiful architectural and natural jewels of the city of the seven hills.
